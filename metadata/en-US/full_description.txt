The Clipboard Manager is a simple Android app for managing clipboard content. It allows users to collect and organize copied text for easy access later.

Features:
- Clipboard Monitoring: The app monitors the device's clipboard to detect copied text.
- Save Text: Copied texts are automatically saved in the app for future reference.
- Delete Text: Users can delete items from the list of saved texts, keeping the clipboard clean and organized.
- Intuitive Interface: A simple and intuitive user interface enables easy and quick navigation.
